Peoplevox

  • 20 January 2019
  • Duration: 3 months
  • Project Length: 2 weeks (avg.)
  • Tools: Sketch
  • Skills: User Research, UX Critique, UI Design

    • Background

    I started at Peoplevox in November, with the responsibility of implementing the design thinking process at the company. The company was developing an all-in-one solution for a collaborative supply chain (CSC) management tool. The product itself was quite mature, but required a lot of UX and UI focus, as this had not been prioritised initially. My role was to implement the design thinking process, and try to embed a user-focused mentality within our work. I took on multiple projects to improve the CSC tool, and I have documented all of my work here on Google Drive.

    The Design Thinking Process, adapted for the company and the supply chain industry. This process was optimised for rapid development, with instances where client interviews were conducted on Monday and features were pushed out on Wednesday.

    As there was an existing system (1 year in development), I developed a deck of issues that were present in terms of the UX and UI in the system. The UX breakdown can be found here, and the UI breakdown here.
